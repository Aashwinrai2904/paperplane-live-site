How it works

1

Courier posts a route

Someone already traveling enters where they are going, when they’re leaving, and how much detour they allow.

2

Sender creates a request

The sender adds pickup, dropoff, package size, and timing. PaperPlane looks for compatible routes.

3

Secure handoff

Pickup and delivery use a QR or passcode verification flow, plus live tracking and status updates.

Why this is different

Not a normal courier app

Traditional apps dispatch someone mainly for the delivery. PaperPlane layers a package onto movement that was already happening.

Better economics

That means lower delivery cost for senders, small earnings for travelers, and fewer unnecessary trips on the road.
